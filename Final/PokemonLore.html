General Lore and Mythos

The stages of the pokemon mythos are as follows:

  1. The creation of the universe. Time, Space, and Antimatter.
  2. Earth is created and protected.
  3. Pangea gets split apart from via the golems.
  4. Life comes to be and so does death. There must be balance.
  5. Knowledge, Emotions, Willpower. These are what drive Humans to survive.
  6. Truth, Ideals, and the absence of both. One can not exist without the others.
  7. Disasters and the preventions and/or corrections.
    8. and
  8. This thing just wanted to interact with humanity so it did.

Then there were all of the man-made legends/mythicals.



In the beninging, there was just void and an egg. From that egg, hatched Arceus (pronounced Ark-ee-us or Ark-ooz). Arceus, with its thousand arms, then created Palkia(pronounced Pahl-key-uh)- the deity of space, Dialga(pronounced Dee-ahl-guh)- the deity of time, and Giratina(pronounced Gear-uh-teen-uh)- the deity of antimatter. It is currently unknown what Arceus' true form is as each time it has been seen and/or caught, it had ended up just being a puppet or part of itself that is more feasible for the human brain to digest.

According to legend, there was a small issue with the domain of Giratina. As antimatter kept on destroying the universe. Seemingly as punishment, Arceus banished Giratina to a realm seperate from the human realm known as the distortion world, in which Giratina's anti-matter had gained the ability to only create instead of destroy. This allowed Giratina to practically become the god of the distortion world; its ruler. It is also said that whenever an action made in our dimension has certain consequences that happen, the opposite occurs in the distortion world.
